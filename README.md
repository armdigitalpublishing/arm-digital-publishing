# ARM Digital Publishing

**Corporate website for ARM Digital Publishing — a multi-division digital enterprise operating at the intersection of technology, creativity, governance, and human narrative.**

Live site: [armdigitalpublishing.com](https://armdigitalpublishing.com)

---

## About

ARM Digital Publishing is a diversified digital publishing and services company operating four active divisions:

- **ARM Advisory & Works** — Business consulting and AI integration
- **ARM Digital Products & Services** — Digital tools and services at scale
- **ARM AI Governance** — Verified human oversight for clinical AI
- **ARM Digital Estates** — Digital territory acquisition and monetisation

Supported by **Current Future** — the company's flagship publication written for future leaders and emerging professionals.

## Tech

- Pure HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no framework, no build step
- Fonts: Unbounded, DM Sans, DM Mono (Google Fonts)
- Fully responsive — mobile, tablet, desktop
- Deployed via Vercel

---

## Deployment

Deployed automatically via Vercel on every push to `main`.

To run locally, simply open `index.html` in any browser.
